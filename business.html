How it works for a platform

  1. 1

    A customer arrives already verified

    They completed full KYC once, with a specialist verification provider, at a participating platform. Kvick ID never holds their documents; it holds their verified details and the outcome of that check.

  2. 2

    You ask, and the customer consents

    Your platform asks Kvick ID a single question: is this person verified, at the level you require? Nothing is shared until the customer approves the request. You learn nothing, not even whether an account exists, until they say yes. One yes is all it takes: the approval stands until the customer withdraws it, so your later re-checks are covered without asking them again.

  3. 3

    You receive signed evidence

    The customer's verified details (name, date of birth, nationality, residence) and their status: verified, at what level, by which provider, valid until when. All of it cryptographically signed, so you can file it and prove later exactly what you received, and when the customer authorised it.

  4. 4

    You make the decision

    Kvick ID supplies verification evidence. Whether to accept it, and how it fits your onboarding, stays entirely with your platform. We supply the evidence; you make and own the compliance call.

Built in, by design

  • Zero document custody, full document access. The files stay with the specialist provider that checked them, exactly as with your own KYC today, and copies remain available on request for the day your regulator asks. You just carry no extra document store.
  • A recorded consent for every field. The customer sees exactly what they are sharing before they say yes, and you can show your regulator the consent behind every data point you hold.
  • Your customer list stays yours. You learn nothing about other platforms' customers through us, and they learn nothing about yours.
  • Nobody can use us as a directory. A request about any email looks identical from the outside until the customer approves it, so accounts cannot be fished for.

Why platforms work with us

Neutral by design
No platform gets preferential treatment, and none learns about another's customers through us. Neutrality is what makes a shared verification layer trustworthy, so it is built in, not promised.
European by design
All personal data is hosted in EU data centres, and the operating company is being established in the EU. Built around EU AML and GDPR requirements from the first line of code.
No document custody
Documents stay with the specialist provider that checked them, available on request when your process requires a copy. Kvick ID holds the verified details and the outcome, never the files, so relying on us does not hand you a new pile of sensitive documents to guard.
Provable, not just asserted
Everything shared is cryptographically signed and tied to a recorded consent, so what was shared, and when, is verifiable later by either side.

Where the line sits. Kvick ID is a verification evidence source. It does not perform, outsource, or replace your KYC or AML programme, and it does not make onboarding decisions for you. Your platform relies on the signed evidence for its own decision, and that decision, along with responsibility for it, stays with you. We are building toward the requirements a regulated platform has to meet, but we make no compliance claims on your behalf.
